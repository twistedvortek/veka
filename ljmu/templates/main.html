App Aim

Sayara aims to revolutionize road safety and efficiency by integrating advanced driver assistance features with comprehensive vehicle tracking for companies. My goal is to leverage cutting-edge technology to enhance driving experiences and streamline fleet management.

Purpose

The purpose of Sayara is to address critical needs in road safety and fleet management by providing data and analytics through cameras and sensors. By doing so, we aim to prevent accidents, optimize driver performance, and contribute to safer road environments.

Scope

Sayara is designed for individual drivers seeking enhanced safety features and companies looking to improve their fleet management. The app includes functionalities like vehicle tracking, driver monitoring, and data analytics, making it versatile for various users and scenarios.

Requirements

To use Sayara effectively, a minimum setup of a 4-core CPU and 8GB RAM is required, with a recommended setup of a 6-core CPU, 16GB RAM, and an Nvidia graphics card for optimal performance. The backend requires Python with support for YOLOv8 and DeepSORT, while the frontend can be managed through any standard web development IDE.

How It Works

Sayara works by capturing and analyzing data from vehicle-mounted cameras and sensors. This information is processed using AI algorithms like YOLOv8 and DeepSORT for object detection and tracking, providing actionable insights and alerts to drivers and fleet managers through a user-friendly dashboard.
